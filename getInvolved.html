Jen's Perspective: Every Life Has Value

The inspiration for the Turtle Shelter Project came from Jen, who knows firsthand the struggles of homelessness. She recalls how difficult even the simplest tasks became when she was cold, wet, and miserable. For many, homelessness isn’t just about losing a place to stay; it’s about losing confidence, energy, hope, and a sense of dignity. Jen shared her experience:

"Emergency preparedness is all about getting ready for disasters, but what about those who are living in their disaster right now? For many who are homeless, life is already an emergency. I know from experience how overwhelming and defeating it feels. Every task becomes harder when you’re cold and wet. This foam clothing can relieve some of that suffering. It doesn’t have to be expensive to make, and it can save lives."

The Turtle Shelter vest is our solution to provide life-saving warmth to those in need without the high cost typically associated with this technology. It’s not just about warmth—it’s about giving people a chance to survive, recover, and regain hope.
